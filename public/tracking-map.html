4. What Gets Tracked (Every Event)

Lead Form Submission

  • Who: student name, parent name, phone, email, school, grade, city
  • Source: traffic_source (google, instagram, whatsapp, direct, etc.)
  • Attribution: utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, utm_content, ad_id
  • Context: landing_page (/lp/1-5), form_open_source (which CTA button), referrer_raw
  • Where it goes: Google Sheet "Lead Log" + ClickUp task + Gmail + WhatsApp

Workshop Funnel Events

  • Page View: workshop_page_view fires when /workshop loads
  • Checkout Start: workshop_checkout_started fires on CTA click
  • Thank You: workshop_thank_you_view fires after Razorpay redirect
  • Each event captures: traffic source, UTMs, ad_id, CTA source (hero/mid/closing/mobile), timestamp
  • Where it goes: Google Sheet "Workshop Funnel"

Workshop Payment

  • Payment ID: Razorpay payment_id, order_id, signature
  • Amount: Rs 3,999 INR
  • Attribution: All UTMs + ad_id + traffic_source recovered from session
  • Pixel events: GA purchase + Meta Purchase + CAPI (server-side)
  • Where it goes: Google Sheet "Workshop Orders" + Meta receives the purchase signal for ad optimization

Meta Campaign Metrics

  • Campaign level: spend, impressions, reach, frequency, clicks, CTR, CPC, CPM, leads, purchases, ROAS
  • Ad level: 47 individual ads with spend, clicks, results breakdown
  • Daily trend: 30-day spend/click/result history
  • Demographics: age + gender breakdown of ad spend
  • Placements: Instagram Feed, Stories, Facebook Feed, Audience Network, etc.
  • Auto-classified: Workshop Leads Events Engagement
5. Dashboard Tabs Explained

Leads

  • Traffic source breakdown (where leads come from)
  • Landing page performance (which LP converts best)
  • CTA button performance (which button on page works best)
  • City distribution

Ad Performance

  • Leads by ad creative (utm_content)
  • Leads by messaging variant (m1-m5)
  • Leads by tone (myth_busting, authority, etc.)
  • Ad ID breakdown

Workshop Pipeline

  • Page views → checkout starts → paid orders (funnel)
  • Conversion rate at each step
  • Which CTA button drives most checkouts
  • Traffic sources for workshop visitors

Meta Ad Spend

  • Total spend, reach, frequency, CPL, CPA, ROAS
  • Category breakdown (workshop/leads/events/engagement)
  • Campaign table with all metrics
  • Top 30 individual ads ranked by spend
  • Daily spend sparkline (30 days)
  • Placement + demographics breakdown
6. Tech Stack

Frontend Tracking

  • Google Analytics (GA4): G-T46NRQ65FV on all pages
  • Meta Pixel: 1606881963979917 on main site, 26011379345197572 on LP pages
  • Meta CAPI: Server-side events via /api/meta-capi for dedup + ad blocker resilience

Backend Pipeline

  • Google Sheets: Lead Log, Workshop Funnel, Workshop Orders tabs
