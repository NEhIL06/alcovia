Problem Amplification
Why bright teenagers still struggle
The issue is rarely intelligence. It is that many teenagers are never pushed to handle pressure, ownership, discomfort, or execution.
Good grades can signal false readiness. A teenager may perform well in school and still find it hard to deal with setbacks, time management, or real responsibility.
Ideas are easy. Execution is rare. Many teenagers talk well. Few are able to finish what they start.
Comfort can weaken capability. When teenagers get rewarded for being in their comfort zone, they grow polished on marksheet but hesitant in practice.
What sets exceptional teenagers apart is how early they build real-world readiness.
