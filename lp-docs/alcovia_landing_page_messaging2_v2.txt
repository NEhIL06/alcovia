Problem Amplification
Section Heading: Why the old playbook no longer works
Intro: Most career guidance asks teenagers to choose early, with too little context and too much anxiety. That may have worked in a more predictable world. It does not work now.
Block 1: College strategy is not career clarity. A strong application may help a teenager get into a university. It does not help them understand what kind of work, environment, or future actually fits them.
Block 2: Outdated advice creates false confidence. Many teenagers are still being prepared for careers that will not exist in 5 years.
Block 3: Too many choices are made without real exposure. Teenagers are pushed to decide before they have seen enough of how real professionals think, work, and build.
Bridge Line: Real clarity comes from learning how tech and AI is changing the career landscape.
